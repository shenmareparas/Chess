# Google Play Games Services Achievement Prompts & Icons Reference

This guide documents the design prompts used to generate consistent achievement icons for your Play Console setup, as well as the corresponding filenames saved in the `screenshots/` directory.

## Master Design Theme
To maintain 100% design consistency, all icons use a unified base layout structure:
- **Base Style:** Premium flat 2D vector mobile game achievement badge
- **Emblem Frame:** Clean minimalist solid gold emblem inside a thick golden circle frame
- **Background:** Solid dark forest green (#0F1B15)
- **Framing:** Full-bleed, no white margins, no white corners, no white spots, no gradients
- **Specs:** 512x512 pixels, 32-bit PNG (under 1 MB)
